# 0001 - Cornerstone is the shared organizational context layer

- **Status:** Accepted

## Context

The product needs one durable identity that stays stable even as integrations, transports, and implementation details evolve.

## Decision

Cornerstone is the shared organizational context layer for humans and AI. It reconstructs organizational meaning, relationships, decision context, and trust disclosure on top of source memory.

## Consequences

- The product is not framed as a source-system replacement.
- The product is not framed as a model-specific assistant.
- Docs and implementation must preserve a common truth layer for humans and AI.
