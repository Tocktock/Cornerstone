# VS5 External Session Records

Create one completed JSON record per participant by copying `../external-session.template.json`.

Required naming: `session-01.json` through `session-05.json` (additional failed or repeated rounds may use a dated suffix). Blank templates are not evidence.

Each completed record must document a non-owner participant with no prior CornerStone exposure, unaided timing, Brief creation, citation inspection, participant restatement, trust/usefulness ratings, willingness to forward or use, and observer or consented recording evidence. At least one of the five sessions must use a real participant decision case. Keep sensitive source text and raw recording data outside this repository; store only redacted references.
